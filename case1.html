Case File No. 7

The Velvet Curtain

A Theatre District Mystery

The Situation

Opening night at the Aldous Theatre ended in tragedy. Mr. Edmund Vance, the theatre's owner, was found dead behind the velvet curtain shortly after the final bow — struck once, cleanly, with the bronze star award kept backstage.

The curtain fell at 9:47 PM. By 9:52, the body was found. In those five minutes, four people were known to be backstage: the leading actress, the stagehand, the playwright, and the theatre critic.

Question them. One of them is lying — and the truth doesn't always announce itself. Listen for what doesn't add up, between what they tell you and what the others say.
