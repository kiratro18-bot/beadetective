Case File No. 15

The Penthouse on 9th

A Disappearance, Not Yet a Crime

The Situation

Iris Calloway, founder and CEO of a mid-sized fintech startup, was last seen entering her ninth-floor apartment at 10:40 PM. By 8:00 AM the next morning — three hours before a board vote that would have stripped her of control of the company she built — she was gone.

The door was locked from the inside. Nothing was broken. Her phone, her passport, and one suitcase were missing; everything else, down to the coffee still cooling on the counter, was exactly where she'd left it. No struggle. No note.

Five people knew her well enough to have a key, a motive, or both: her co-founder, her assistant, the building's night doorman, her ex-fiancé, and her lawyer. Nobody's reported a crime, exactly. But somebody knows more than they're saying, and the board vote is in six hours.

Find out if Iris ran, or if she was made to disappear — and who helped, or hindered, either way. You only get one name.
